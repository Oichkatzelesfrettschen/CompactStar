\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Sequence point for compact-\/star M–R sequences and diagnostics. 

A lightweight POD-\/style container for a single model point along a stellar sequence (e.\+g., fixed EOS, varying central density). Stores commonly used scalars such as energy density, mass, radius, central pressure, baryon number, and moment of inertia.
