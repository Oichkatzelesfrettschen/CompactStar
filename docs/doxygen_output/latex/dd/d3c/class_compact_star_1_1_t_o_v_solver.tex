\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em f} & Factor by which the minimum EOS energy density is multiplied to form the {\itshape lowest} allowed central energy density in Solve(...).\\

If the EOS minimum energy density is $\varepsilon_{\min}$, then the solver will clamp user inputs to at least $ \varepsilon_{\text{floor}} = f \times \varepsilon_{\min} $.

This is to avoid trying to build stars whose {\itshape central} energy density is already too close to the surface value of the EOS, which typically makes the TOV integration stop immediately.
