High-\/level baryon-\/number-\/violation utilities. 

Contains spin-\/down limits, observational constraints, and wrappers that connect pulsar quantities to \mbox{\hyperlink{namespace_compact_star_1_1_physics_1_1_b_n_v}{BNV}} reaction models in Microphysics.
