\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Driver that converts chemical free energy (η⋅Γ) into thermal heating. 

Contributes to d\+T̃/dt via Q\+\_\+heat = Σ\+\_\+i Γ\+\_\+i(η, T̃, …) $\ast$ η\+\_\+i, distributed over the stellar volume and normalized by the effective heat capacity. Chemical {\itshape evolution} (dη/dt) is handled by separate drivers (e.\+g., Weak\+Restoration).
