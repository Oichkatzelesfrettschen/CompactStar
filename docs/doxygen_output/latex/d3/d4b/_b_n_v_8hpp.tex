Computes an upper limit on $\Gamma_{\mathrm{BNV}}$ by requiring the BNV heating power not to exceed the observed spin-\/down power (model assumptions apply).

Compute the spin-\/down bound and store it in a BNVState block. 

Convenience wrapper around Spin\+Down\+Limit(...) that writes the resulting bound into the BNV evolution/diagnostic state. This keeps BNV-\/related quantities available to drivers and observers without recomputing them.

By convention, the spin-\/down limit is stored in bnv.\+Spin\+Down\+Limit() matching the accessor provided by Physics\+::\+State\+::\+BNVState.
