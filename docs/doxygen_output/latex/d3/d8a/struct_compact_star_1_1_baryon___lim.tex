\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Tracks baryon number violation (BNV) constraints for a species. 

Models the fractional abundance and BNV rate of a baryon species within the star, including gravitational redshift of rates and experimental/theoretical limits.

Physical definitions\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item fraction\+: number density fraction of species relative to total baryon density.
\item b\+\_\+dot\+: proper-\/time baryon number change rate including redshift factor e$^\wedge$\{nu(r)\}, accounting for slower clocks deeper in the gravitational well.
\item limit\+: upper bound on BNV rate from particle physics experiments (e.\+g., di-\/nucleon decay). 
\end{DoxyItemize}
