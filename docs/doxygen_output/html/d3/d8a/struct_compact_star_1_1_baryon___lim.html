Detailed Description

Tracks baryon number violation (BNV) constraints for a species.

Models the fractional abundance and BNV rate of a baryon species within the star, including gravitational redshift of rates and experimental/theoretical limits.

Physical definitions:

  • fraction: number density fraction of species relative to total baryon density.
  • b_dot: proper-time baryon number change rate including redshift factor e^{nu(r)}, accounting for slower clocks deeper in the gravitational well.
  • limit: upper bound on BNV rate from particle physics experiments (e.g., di-nucleon decay).
